# Girls Who Lead @ Pitt
Girls Who Lead @ Pitt (GWL), affiliated with the national nonprofit Her Rising Initiative, exists to close the opportunity gap for women in leadership. We bring together students who are interested in policy, law, business, technology, and advocacy and we give them the spaces, skills, and connections to actually do something with those interests.
